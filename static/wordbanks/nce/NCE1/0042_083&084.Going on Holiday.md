Lesson 83

Going on holiday

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

Where did Sam go for his holiday this year?
*萨姆今年去哪里度假了？*

Hello, Sam.
*你好，萨姆。*

Come in.
*进来。*

Hi, Sam.
*嗨，萨姆。*

We're having lunch.
*我们正在吃午饭。*

Do you want to have lunch with us?
*你想和我们一起吃午饭吗？*

No, thank you, Tom.
*不用了，谢谢你，汤姆。*

I've already had lunch.
*我已经吃过午饭了。*

I had lunch at half past twelve.
*我十二点半吃午饭的。*

Have a cup of coffee then.
*那喝杯咖啡吧。*

I've just had a cup, thank you.
*我刚喝了一杯，谢谢。*

I had one after my lunch.
*我午饭后喝的。*

Let's go into the living room, Carol.
*凯罗尔，我们去客厅吧。*

We can have our coffee there.
*我们可以在那儿喝咖啡。*

Excuse the mess, Sam.
*萨姆，请原谅这儿有点乱。*

This room's very untidy.
*这房间太不整洁了。*

We're packing our suitcases.
*我们正在打包行李箱。*

We're going to leave tomorrow.
*我们明天要走了。*

Tom and I are going to have a holiday.
*汤姆和我要去度假了。*

Aren't you lucky!
*你们真幸运！*

When are you going to have a holiday, Sam?
*萨姆，你什么时候去度假？*

I don't know.
*我不知道。*

I've already had my holiday this year.
*我今年已经度过假了。*

Where did you go?
*你去哪儿了？*

I stayed at home!
*我待在家里！*
