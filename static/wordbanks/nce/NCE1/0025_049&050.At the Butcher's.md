# 049&050.At the Butcher's

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 49
*第49课*

At the butcher's
*在肉店*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答问题。*

What does Mr. Bird like?
*伯德先生喜欢什么？*

Do you want any meat today, Mrs. Bird?
*伯德夫人，您今天想买些肉吗？*

Yes, please.
*是的，请给我。*

Do you want beef or lamb?
*您想要牛肉还是羊肉？*

Beef, please.
*请给我牛肉。*

This lamb's very good.
*这羊肉很好。*

I like lamb, but my husband doesn't.
*我喜欢羊肉，但我丈夫不喜欢。*

What about some steak?
*来点牛排怎么样？*

This is a nice piece.
*这块很好。*

Give me that piece, please.
*请把那块给我。*

And a pound of mince, too.
*还有一磅肉末。*

Do you want a chicken, Mrs. Bird?
*伯德夫人，您要只鸡吗？*

They're very nice.
*这些鸡很好。*

No, thank you.
*不，谢谢。*

My husband likes steak,
*我丈夫喜欢牛排，*

but he doesn't like chicken.
*但他不喜欢鸡肉。*

To tell you the truth, Mrs. Bird,
*说实话，伯德夫人，*

I don't like chicken either!
*我也不喜欢鸡肉！*
