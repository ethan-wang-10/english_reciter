# 067&068.The Weekend

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 67
*第67课*

The weekend
*周末*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

What are the Johnsons going to do at the weekend?
*约翰逊一家周末打算做什么？*

Hello. Were you at the butcher's?
*你好。你刚才在肉店吗？*

Yes, I was.
*是的，我在那里。*

Were you at the butcher's, too?
*你也在肉店吗？*

No, I wasn't.
*不，我不在。*

I was at the greengrocer's.
*我在蔬菜店。*

How's Jimmy today?
*吉米今天怎么样？*

He's very well, thank you.
*他很好，谢谢你。*

Was he absent from school last week?
*他上周没去上学吗？*

Yes, he was.
*是的，他没去。*

He was absent on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday.
*他周一、周二、周三和周四都没去。*

How are you all keeping?
*你们都好吗？*

Very well, thank you.
*我们都很好，谢谢。*

We're going to spend three days in the country!
*我们要去乡下待三天！*

We're going to stay at my mother's for the weekend.
*这个周末我们要待在我妈妈家。*

Friday, Saturday and Sunday in the country!
*周五、周六和周日在乡下！*

Aren't you lucky!
*你们真幸运！*
