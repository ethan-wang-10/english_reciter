# 097&098.A Small Blue Case

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 97
*第97课*

A small blue case
*一个蓝色小箱子*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

Does Mr. Hall get his case back?
*霍尔先生拿回他的箱子了吗？*

I left a suitcase on the train to London the other day.
*前几天我把一个手提箱忘在去伦敦的火车上了。*

Can you describe it, sir?
*先生，您能描述一下它吗？*

It's a small blue case and it's got a zip.
*那是一个蓝色小箱子，有拉链。*

There's a label on the handle with my name and address on it.
*把手上有一个标签，上面有我的姓名和地址。*

Is this case yours?
*这个箱子是您的吗？*

No, that's not mine.
*不，那不是我的。*

What about this one?
*这个呢？*

This one's got a label.
*这个上面有标签。*

Let me see it.
*让我看看。*

What's your name and address?
*您的姓名和地址是什么？*

David Hall, 83, Bridge Street.
*大卫·霍尔，桥街83号。*

That's right.
*对。*

D. N. Hall, 83, Bridge Street.
*D. N. 霍尔，桥街83号。*

Three pounds fifty pence, please.
*请付三英镑五十便士。*

Here you are.
*给你。*

Thank you.
*谢谢。*

Hey!
*嘿！*

What's the matter?
*怎么了？*

This case doesn't belong to me!
*这个箱子不是我的！*

You've given me the wrong case!
*你把箱子给我弄错了！*
