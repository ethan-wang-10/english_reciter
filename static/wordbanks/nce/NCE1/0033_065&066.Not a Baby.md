# 065&066.Not a Baby

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 65
*第65课*

Not a baby
*不是小孩子*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答问题。*

Does Jill take the key to the front door?
*吉尔拿走了前门的钥匙吗？*

What are you going to do this evening, Jill?
*吉尔，你今晚打算做什么？*

I'm going to meet some friends, Dad.
*爸爸，我打算去见几个朋友。*

You mustn't come home late.
*你不许回来太晚。*

You must be home at half past ten.
*你必须在十点半之前到家。*

I can't get home so early, Dad!
*爸爸，我不能那么早回家！*

Can I have the key to the front door, please?
*我能拿前门的钥匙吗？*

No, you can't.
*不，你不能。*

Jill's eighteen years old, Tom.
*吉尔已经十八岁了，汤姆。*

She's not a baby.
*她不是小孩子。*

Give her the key.
*把钥匙给她吧。*

She always comes home early.
*她总是回家很早的。*

Oh, all right!
*哦，好吧！*

Here you are.
*给你。*

But you mustn't come home after a quarter past eleven.
*但是你不能在十一点一刻之后回家。*

Do you hear?
*听到了吗？*

Yes, Dad.
*是的，爸爸。*

Thanks, Mum.
*谢谢，妈妈。*

That's all right.
*没关系。*

Goodbye.
*再见。*

Enjoy yourself!
*玩得开心！*

We always enjoy ourselves, Mum.
*妈妈，我们总是玩得很开心的。*

Bye-bye.
*再见。*
