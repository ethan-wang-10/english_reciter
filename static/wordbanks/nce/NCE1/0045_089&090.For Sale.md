Lesson 89
*第89课*

For sale
*待售*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

Why couldn't Nigel decide?
*为什么奈杰尔不能决定？*

Good afternoon.
*下午好。*

I believe that this house is for sale.
*我想这栋房子是待售的。*

That's right.
*没错。*

May I have a look at it, please?
*我能进去看看吗？*

Yes, of course.
*当然可以。*

Come in.
*请进。*

How long have you lived here?
*你在这里住了多久了？*

I've lived here for twenty years.
*我在这里住了二十年了。*

Twenty years!
*二十年了！*

That's a long time.
*那可真久啊。*

Yes, I've been here since 1976.
*是的，我从1976年就住在这里了。*

Then why do you want to sell it?
*那你为什么要卖掉它呢？*

Because I've just retired.
*因为我刚退休。*

I want to buy a small house in the country.
*我想在乡下买一栋小房子。*

How much does this house cost?
*这栋房子多少钱？*

￡68, 500.
*68,500英镑。*

That's a lot of money!
*那可真是一大笔钱！*

It's worth every penny of it.
*它值这个价。*

Well, I like the house, but I can't decide yet.
*嗯，我喜欢这栋房子，但我还不能决定。*

My wife must see it first.
*我妻子必须先看看。*

Women always have the last word.
*女人总是说了算。*
