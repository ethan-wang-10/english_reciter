# 059&060.Is That All

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 59
*第59课*

Is that all?
*就这些吗？*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答问题。*

Does the lady buy any chalk?
*这位女士买粉笔了吗？*

I want some envelopes, please.
*我要一些信封。*

Do you want the large size or the small size?
*您要大的还是小的？*

The large size, please.
*请给我大的。*

Do you have any writing paper?
*您有信纸吗？*

Yes, we do.
*有，我们有。*

I don't have any small pads.
*我没有小本的。*

I only have large ones.
*我只有大本的。*

Do you want a pad?
*您要一本吗？*

Yes, please.
*是的，谢谢。*

And I want some glue.
*我还要一些胶水。*

A bottle of glue.
*一瓶胶水。*

And I want a large box of chalk, too.
*我还要一大盒粉笔。*

I only have small boxes.
*我只有小盒的。*

Do you want one?
*您要一盒吗？*

No, thank you.
*不，谢谢。*

Is that all?
*就这些吗？*

That's all, thank you.
*就这些，谢谢。*

What else do you want?
*您还想要什么？*

I want my change.
*我要我的零钱。*
