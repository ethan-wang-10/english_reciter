# 003&004.Sorry, Sir

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 3
*第3课*

Sorry sir.
*对不起，先生。*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

Does the man get his umbrella back?
*这位男士找回他的伞了吗？*

My coat and my umbrella please.
*请把我的大衣和雨伞给我。*

Here is my ticket.
*这是我的票。*

Thank you sir.
*谢谢您，先生。*

Number five.
*五号。*

Here's your umbrella and your coat.
*这是您的伞和大衣。*

This is not my umbrella.
*这不是我的伞。*

Sorry sir.
*对不起，先生。*

Is this your umbrella?
*这是您的伞吗？*

No it isn't.
*不，它不是。*

Is this it?
*是这把吗？*

Yes it is.
*是的，就是它。*

Thank you very much.
*非常感谢。*
