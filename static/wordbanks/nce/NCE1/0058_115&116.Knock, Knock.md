# 115&116.Knock, Knock

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 115
*第115课*

Knock, knock!
*敲敲门！*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

What does Jim have to drink?
*吉姆喝什么？*

Isn't there anyone at home?
*家里没人吗？*

I'll knock again, Helen.
*海伦，我再敲一次。*

Everything's very quiet.
*一切都很安静。*

I'm sure there's no one at home.
*我确信家里没人。*

But that's impossible.
*但那不可能。*

Carol and Tom invited us to lunch.
*卡罗尔和汤姆邀请我们吃午饭。*

Look through the window.
*看看窗户。*

Can you see anything?
*你能看到什么吗？*

Nothing at all.
*什么都没有。*

Let's try the back door.
*我们试试后门吧。*

Look! Everyone's in the garden.
*看！大家都在花园里。*

Everybody wants to have lunch in the garden.
*大家都想在花园里吃午饭。*

It's nice and warm out here.
*这里又好又暖和。*

Come and have something to drink.
*来喝点什么吧。*

Thanks, Carol.
*谢谢，卡罗尔。*

May I have a glass of beer please?
*请给我一杯啤酒好吗？*

Beer?
*啤酒？*

There's none left.
*没剩下了。*

You can have some lemonade.
*你可以喝些柠檬水。*

Lemonade!
*柠檬水！*

Don't believe her, Jim.
*别信她，吉姆。*

She's only joking.
*她只是开玩笑。*

Have some beer!
*喝点啤酒！*
