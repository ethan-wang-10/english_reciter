# 105&106.Full of Mistakes

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 105
*第105课*

Full of mistakes
*错误百出*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

What was Sandra's present?
*桑德拉的礼物是什么？*

Where's Sandra, Bob?
*桑德拉在哪里，鲍勃？*

I want her.
*我找她。*

Do you want to speak to her?
*你想和她说话吗？*

Yes, I do.
*是的，我想。*

I want her to come to my office.
*我要她到我办公室来。*

Tell her to come at once.
*告诉她马上过来。*

Did you want to see me?
*你想见我吗？*

Ah, yes, Sandra.
*啊，是的，桑德拉。*

How do you spell 'intelligent'?
*'intelligent'怎么拼写？*

Can you tell me?
*你能告诉我吗？*

I-N-T-E-L-L-I-G-E-N-T.
*I-N-T-E-L-L-I-G-E-N-T。*

That's right.
*对了。*

You've typed it with only one 'L'.
*你只打了一个'L'。*

This letter's full of mistakes.
*这封信错误百出。*

I want you to type it again.
*我要你再打一遍。*

Yes, I'll do that.
*好的，我会的。*

I'm sorry about that.
*对此我很抱歉。*

And here's a little present for you.
*这是给你的一份小礼物。*

What is it?
*这是什么？*

It's a dictionary.
*是本词典。*

I hope it'll help you.
*我希望它能帮到你。*
