# 077&078.Terrible Toothache

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 77
*第77课*

Terrible toothache
*严重的牙痛*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音带，然后回答这个问题。*

What time of day is it, do you think? How do you know?
*你认为现在是几点？你是怎么知道的？*

Good morning, Mr. Croft.
*早上好，克罗夫特先生。*

Good morning, nurse.
*早上好，护士。*

I want to see the dentist, please.
*我想看牙医。*

Do you have an appointment?
*你预约了吗？*

No, I don't.
*没有。*

Is it urgent?
*情况紧急吗？*

Yes, it is.
*是的，很紧急。*

It's very urgent.
*非常紧急。*

I feel awful.
*我感觉糟透了。*

I have a terrible toothache.
*我牙痛得厉害。*

Can you come at 10 a.m. on Monday, April 24th?
*你能在4月24日星期一上午10点来吗？*

I must see the dentist now, nurse.
*护士，我必须现在就看牙医。*

The dentist is very busy at the moment.
*牙医现在很忙。*

Can you come at 2 p.m. ?
*你能在下午2点来吗？*

That's very late.
*那太晚了。*

Can the dentist see me now?
*牙医现在能给我看病吗？*

I'm afraid that he can't, Mr. Croft.
*克罗夫特先生，恐怕他不能。*

Can't you wait till this afternoon?
*你不能等到今天下午吗？*

I can wait, but my toothache can't!
*我能等，但我的牙痛不能等！*
