# 125&126.Tea for Two

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 125
*第125课*

Tea for two
*两人喝茶*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答问题。*

Does Susan have tea by herself?
*苏珊独自一人喝茶吗？*

Can't you come in and have tea now, Peter?
*彼得，你现在不能进来喝茶吗？*

Not yet.
*还不能。*

I must water the garden first.
*我必须先浇花园。*

Do you have to water it now?
*你现在非浇不可吗？*

I'm afraid I must.
*恐怕非浇不可。*

Look at it!
*你瞧！*

It's terribly dry.
*干得厉害。*

What a nuisance!
*真讨厌！*

Last summer it was very dry, too.
*去年夏天也是很干。*

Don't you remember?
*你不记得了吗？*

I had to water it every day.
*我不得不每天浇水。*

Well, I'll have tea by myself.
*哎，我一个人喝茶了。*

That was quick!
*真快！*

Have you finished already?
*你已经浇完了吗？*

Yes.
*是的。*

Look out of the window.
*你往窗外看。*

It's raining!
*下雨了！*

That means you don't need to water the garden.
*这意味着你不用浇花园了。*

That was a pleasant surprise.
*这真是个意外的惊喜。*

It means I can have tea, instead.
*这意味着我可以去喝茶了。*
