# 113&114.Small Change

**Book:** NCE (NCE1)

---

Lesson 113
*第113课*

Small change
*零钱*

Listen to the tape then answer this question.
*听录音，然后回答这个问题。*

Who has got some small change?
*谁有零钱？*

Fares, please!
*请买票！*

Trafalgar Square, please.
*请到特拉法尔加广场。*

I'm sorry, sir.
*对不起，先生。*

I can't change a ten-pound note.
*我没法找开这张十英镑的钞票。*

Haven't you got any small change?
*你没有零钱吗？*

I've got no small change,
*我没有零钱，*

I am afraid.
*恐怕是这样。*

I'll ask some of the passengers.
*我去问问其他乘客。*

Have you any small change, sir?
*先生，您有零钱吗？*

I'm sorry.
*对不起。*

I've got none.
*我没有。*

I haven't got any either.
*我也没有。*

Can you change this ten-pound note, madam?
*女士，您能找开这张十英镑的钞票吗？*

I'm afraid I can't.
*恐怕我不能。*

Neither can I.
*我也不能。*

I'm very sorry, sir.
*我非常抱歉，先生。*

You must get off the bus.
*您必须下车。*

None of our passengers can change this note.
*我们没有一个乘客能找开这张钞票。*

They're all millionaires!
*他们都是百万富翁！*

Except us.
*除了我们。*

I've got some small change.
*我有一些零钱。*

So have I.
*我也有。*
