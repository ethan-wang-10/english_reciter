# 08.The Best and the Worst

**Book:** NCE (NCE2)

---

Why is Joe's garden
*为什么乔的花园*

the most beautiful one in the town?
*是镇上最漂亮的一个？*

Joe Sanders has the most beautiful garden
*乔·桑德斯拥有最美丽的花园*

in our town.
*在我们镇上。*

Nearly everybody enters for
*几乎每个人都报名参加*

'The Nicest Garden Competition' each year,
*每年的“最美花园竞赛”，*

but Joe wins every time.
*但乔每次都赢。*

Bill Frith's garden is larger than Joe's.
*比尔·弗里斯的花园比乔的花园大。*

Bill works harder than Joe
*比尔比乔更努力工作*

and grows more flowers and vegetables,
*并且种植更多的花卉和蔬菜，*

but Joe's garden is more interesting.
*但乔的花园更有趣。*

He has made neat paths
*他修建了整齐的小径*

and has built a wooden bridge over a pool.
*并在池塘上架起了一座木桥。*

I like gardens too,
*我也喜欢花园，*

but I do not like hard work.
*但我不喜欢辛苦劳作。*

Every year
*每年*

I enter for the garden competition too,
*我也参加花园竞赛，*

and I always win a little prize
*我总是赢得一个小奖*

for the worst garden in the town!
*因为我是镇上最差的花园！*
